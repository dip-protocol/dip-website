\# Decision Lifecycle



A decision artifact moves through several stages.



\## 1 Creation



A decision system produces a structured artifact.



\## 2 Canonicalization



The artifact is serialized using canonical JSON.



\## 3 Signing



The artifact hash is signed using Ed25519.



\## 4 Verification



Independent systems verify the signature.



\## 5 Registry Publication



Artifacts may be published to an append-only transparency registry.



\## 6 Audit



Auditors verify artifact integrity and registry chain.
