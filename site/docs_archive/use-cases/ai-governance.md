\# AI Governance



Artificial intelligence systems increasingly make decisions that impact individuals, organizations, and governments.



Examples include:



\- loan approvals

\- fraud detection

\- hiring decisions

\- risk assessment



However, many AI systems lack verifiable decision evidence.



The Decision Integrity Protocol enables AI systems to produce \*\*cryptographically verifiable decision artifacts\*\*.



These artifacts allow:



\- regulators to audit AI behavior

\- organizations to prove model integrity

\- independent parties to verify decision authenticity
