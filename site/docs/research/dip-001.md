\# DIP-001 — Decision Integrity Protocol



Author: \*\*Pavan Dev Singh Charak\*\*  

Year: \*\*2026\*\*



---



\## Abstract



The Decision Integrity Protocol (DIP) introduces a framework for producing

cryptographically verifiable decision artifacts. The protocol enables

deterministic governance systems to generate transparent and auditable

decision records.



---



\## Introduction



Modern governance systems often lack verifiable evidence of how decisions

are produced. The Decision Integrity Protocol addresses this problem by

introducing a standardized method for recording decisions as signed

artifacts.



---



\## Architecture



The protocol defines three core components:



1\. Decision Artifact  

2\. Decision Ledger  

3\. Independent Verification Engine



These components enable reproducible decision pipelines and transparent

auditability.



---



\## Conclusion



The Decision Integrity Protocol establishes a foundation for deterministic

governance infrastructure and verifiable decision systems.
