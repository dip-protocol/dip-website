\# Use Cases for Verifiable Decision Systems



The Decision Integrity Protocol enables verifiable decision infrastructure across multiple industries.



As automation expands, the ability to independently verify decisions becomes critical.



This section describes how DIP can be applied across different domains.



\## Key Areas



\- Artificial Intelligence Governance

\- Financial Systems

\- Regulatory Compliance

\- Autonomous Systems

\- Digital Governance



These domains increasingly rely on automated decision systems that must produce verifiable evidence.
